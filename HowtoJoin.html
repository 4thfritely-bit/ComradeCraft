How to join ComradeCraft

A Step by Step Guide!

  1. Open Minecraft on the latest version
    Disclaimer: ComradeCraft may not always be on the latest version, to check, open on the latest version, you will then see if you need to re-launch on a different version if you attempt to join and it tells you otherwise, please launch on that version
  2. Press the "Add Server" Button and type this domain: ComradeCraft.aternos.me
  3. If the server is online, you will be able to join it!
